Content / SEO: highest value per affiliate

Despite being the smallest non-ads group (4,775), Content/SEO publishers have the highest avg GMV ($42,350) and AOV ($230) — nearly 3x the avg of Social/Creator. Their organic search traffic carries the highest purchase intent. Investing in this type yields the best per-affiliate ROI, though the pool is smaller and harder to acquire.
